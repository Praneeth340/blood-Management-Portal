Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Anyone who is healthy and meets the donor eligibility criteria can donate blood. You can check the eligibility requirements on our website.

How often can I donate blood?

You can donate blood every 56 days. Platelets can be donated every 7 days.
